02 / Market Map

2 Categories of AI Agent Marketplaces

The Web3 AI agent/skill marketplace splits into 2 relevant categories. ClawFriend straddles both — bonding curve trading (launchpad) + skill marketplace (registry) + social layer. No competitor combines all three on BNB Chain.

Category A: Agent Launchpads

Tokenized agents + trading. Buy/sell agent tokens via bonding curves or DEX.

  • Virtuals Protocol — $39.5M revenue, 18K agents, ACP commerce
  • CreatorBid — 3,500 agents, token bonding — dying, -97%
  • ChatAndBuild — 140K users, BAP-578 NFA standard
  • ClawFriend — bonding curve, BNB — pre-traction
  • Clanker — 21K token launches/day — high volume, low quality

Category B: Skill/Plugin Registries

Capability marketplaces. Developer tools, plugins, skills for AI agent frameworks.

  • ClawHub — 3,286 skills, 1.5M downloads, FREE
  • MyShell — 5M users, 200K+ agents, consumer-friendly
  • ClawFriend — skill marketplace + holder-gated access
ClawFriend's unique position: It straddles Category A + B — bonding curve trading (launchpad) + skill marketplace (registry) + social layer. No competitor combines all three on BNB Chain. This is both the opportunity and the challenge.

09 / Market Stage Analysis

Where Is This Market Right Now?

Market Stage: EARLY GROWTH, COMPRESSING

  • AI agent market cap hit ~$11.3B across 550+ projects but crashed 90% from ATH
  • Leaders consolidating (Virtuals $450M, MyShell 5M users)
  • BNB Chain now has 58 AI projects — no longer uncontested
  • Window of opportunity: 6-9 months before top players lock market share
  • Only 1 platform with proven revenue >$10M (Virtuals: $39.5M)
  • Most platforms launched 2024-2025 = all still young

Who Is Leading?

  • Virtuals Protocol — Clear leader: $39.5M revenue, 650K holders, 5-chain including BNB
  • MyShell — Leads in users (5M) on BNB Chain specifically
  • ChatAndBuild — Fastest-growing newcomer (140K users with BAP-578)
  • Monetization is unsolved — Most registries/stores are free
  • Supply > demand — 200K+ agents (MyShell) but unclear how many earn
  • Room for new entrant? YES, but only with unique positioning

What Gaps Are Unaddressed?

1. Skill Verification / Curation

Post-ClawHavoc, no marketplace offers verified skills. First mover wins trust. 12-20% of ClawHub skills are malicious — nobody addresses it.

2. Holder-Gated Exclusivity

MyShell = freemium, ChatAndBuild = NFA, Virtuals = open. Nobody uses bonding curve shares to gate premium skills.

3. OpenClaw On-Chain Monetization

3,286 skills × $0 revenue. The entire OpenClaw skill ecosystem is still unmonetized.

4. BNB DeFi-Specific Skills

$5.64B TVL, 2.6M daily addresses, no dedicated DeFi skill marketplace on BNB Chain.
